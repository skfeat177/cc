About Credible Classes

Introduction

Credible Classes, established in 2020, is not just an institute but also plays a pivotal role in instilling confidence and building strong fundamentals in students. It stands as one of the leading institutes in Kankarbhag (Patna) for pre-foundation batches.

At Credible Classes, tests are meticulously designed to assess the temperament and potential of students in the fields of medical and engineering. We offer an exceptional system for preparing students for Medical/Engineering exams (NEET/JEE) even during their earlier academic years.

Our institute provides an optimal environment and the necessary resources to ensure that students are well-prepared to excel in competitive exams. Our goal is to secure the selection of students in these highly competitive exams by providing the appropriate level of guidance and support.

Features

  • ➤ Monthly academic test series (ATS) and competitive test series (CTS) to analyze progress of students.
  • ➤ Air-conditioned classroom.
  • ➤ Access to high quality R.O drinking water.
  • ➤ CCTV camera for high security
  • ➤ Easy access to notes and results by students and parents.
  • ➤ Separate batch for academic syllabus and competitive syllabus.
  • ➤ Excellent quality of notes and modules.
  • ➤ Exam answer sheet designed as per boards examination and competitive examination.
  • ➤ Doubt class for each subject.
